        Character Introduction: Zenitsu Agatsuma is one of the main characters in the series. He is a member of the Demon Slayer Corps, fighting against demons to protect humanity.

        Personality: Zenitsu is initially portrayed as a cowardly and anxious character. He often expresses fear and doubt, especially when facing dangerous situations.
        
        Thunder Breathing Techniques: Zenitsu is a practitioner of the Thunder Breathing techniques, a unique swordsmanship style that focuses on lightning-based attacks. Despite his initial cowardice, he displays remarkable skill in combat when motivated.
        
        Nichirin Sword: Like other demon slayers, Zenitsu wields a Nichirin sword, which is effective against demons. His sword has a distinct yellow color.
        
        Seven Forms of Thunder Breathing: Zenitsu masters the first form of Thunder Breathing early in the series and later develops additional forms as he continues to grow as a demon slayer.
        
        Sleeping Technique: Zenitsu has a unique ability called the "Thunderclap and Flash," which is a powerful technique he uses in moments of extreme danger. However, he enters a deep sleep afterward, leaving him unconscious and vulnerable.
        
        Loud Personality: Despite his fearful disposition, Zenitsu is known for his loud and expressive personality, especially when it comes to his admiration for women.
        
        Friendship with Tanjiro: Zenitsu becomes close friends with Tanjiro Kamado during their training and missions as demon slayers. Their camaraderie is an important aspect of the series.
        
        Encounter with Nezuko: Zenitsu has a memorable encounter with Nezuko Kamado, Tanjiro's demon-turned sister, which influences his perception of demons and strengthens his resolve as a demon slayer.
        
        Yellow Cloak: As a member of the Demon Slayer Corps, Zenitsu wears the standard Demon Slayer uniform, including a distinctive yellow cloak.
        
        Character Development: Throughout the series, Zenitsu undergoes significant character development. He gradually overcomes his initial fears and insecurities, becoming a more capable and confident demon slayer.
