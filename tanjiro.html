    Protagonist: Tanjiro is the main protagonist of the series. His journey is central to the plot as he strives to avenge his family and save his demon-turned sister, Nezuko.

    Family Tragedy: Tanjiro's family is attacked by demons, resulting in the death of his parents and the transformation of his sister Nezuko into a demon.
    
    Kind-hearted Nature: Tanjiro is known for his compassionate and kind-hearted nature. Despite the hardships he faces, he maintains empathy and understanding, even towards demons.
    
    Water Breathing Techniques: Tanjiro is a practitioner of the Water Breathing techniques, a swordsmanship style that enhances his combat abilities against demons.
    
    Nichirin Sword: He wields a Nichirin sword, a weapon specially designed to slay demons. His sword is unique with a black blade, which changes color in the presence of different demon blood.
    
    Sense of Smell: Tanjiro possesses an incredibly keen sense of smell, allowing him to detect and analyze scents. This skill is valuable in tracking demons and identifying their weaknesses.
    
    Demon Slayer Corps: Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps, an organization dedicated to eradicating demons and protecting humanity. He undergoes rigorous training to become a skilled demon slayer.
    
    Hanafuda Earrings: Tanjiro wears distinctive hanafuda earrings, which originally belonged to his father. These earrings play a symbolic role in the series.
    
    Companionship: Throughout his journey, Tanjiro forms strong bonds with fellow demon slayers, including Inosuke Hashibira and Zenitsu Agatsuma, as well as his demon-turned sister Nezuko.
    
    Demon Blood Art: Tanjiro later develops a unique ability called the "Hinokami Kagura," a form of the Demon Slayer Mark that grants him enhanced strength and agility.
